Ahungala

Ahungalla is a small coastal town, located in Galle District, Southern Province, Sri Lanka. And is known primarily for its sandy beach, accompanied by a wide palm belt, considered to be one of the best on the island. Ahungalla can be reached by main Colombo-Galle-Matara (A2) motor road as well as by southern railway line.

Ella

Ella is a small town in the Badulla District of Uva Province, Sri Lanka. The area has a rich bio-diversity, dense with numerous varieties of flora and fauna. Ella is surrounded by hills covered with cloud forests and tea plantations. The town has a cooler climate than surrounding lowlands, due to its elevation

Habarana

Habarana is a small city in the Anuradhapura District of Sri Lanka. Habarana is a popular tourist destination for safari lovers as it is the starting point for safaris in the nearby Habarana jungle and the Minneriya sanctuary which is heavily populated by elephants.
